Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. Returns are free within Pakistan.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery within Pakistan takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for next-day delivery in major cities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), credit/debit cards, JazzCash, Easypaisa, and bank transfers.

How can I track my order?

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email to monitor your order status in real-time.
